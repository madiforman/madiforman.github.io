Special Interests

I have physical disability, caused from damage to my spinal cord from childhood cancer. Because of this, in high-school I competitively raced in adaptive track and field. I threw myself into it and was eventually approached by the United States Paralympic track and field coaches, and brought to California to train with them. However, I would’ve had to define my life around it. I would’ve had to push off school to train and focus, and that didn’t feel like me, so now I practice for fun on our campus track.

Outside of that, I am a creative person. I have been drawing and painting since I can remember, and after focusing my studies on computer science, have discovered “creative coding”, and it has slowly been consuming my creative focus.
